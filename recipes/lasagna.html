Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a famous Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

It is rich, delicious, and perfect for dinner.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Boil noodles.
  2. Cook meat and sauce.
  3. Layer noodles and cheese.
  4. Bake for 40 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
