Biryani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with spices, meat, and basmati rice.

It is one of the most popular dishes in India.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Yogurt
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Prepare chicken with spices.
  3. Layer rice and chicken.
  4. Cook on low heat.
  5. Serve hot.
