Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft and fluffy breakfast cakes served with syrup.

They are easy to make and very tasty.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients.
  2. Heat the pan.
  3. Pour batter.
  4. Cook both sides.
  5. Serve with syrup.
